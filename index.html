Leafline NGO Proposal

Executive Summary

South Africa faces a youth employment crisis of historic proportions... Leafline was founded to meet this challenge... Together we can turn despair into purpose for South Africa’s next generation.

Background and Context

South Africa’s youth unemployment rate is among the highest in the world... This vacuum – between existing training programs and the real, human needs of job-seekers – is exactly where Leafline steps in.

The Vision and Mission of Leafline

Leafline envisions a South Africa where every young person... In all our work, Leafline’s values are clear: hope, dignity and purpose.

How Leafline Works

  1. WhatsApp Sign-up: Young people join by messaging Leafline...
  2. Guided Questions: A Leafline facilitator chats with the youth...
  3. Skill Mining: From the conversation we identify hidden assets...
  4. Personalized CV Building: We then rewrite or reorganize the CV...
  5. Empowerment Conversation: Beyond the CV, every Leafline session is also a mentoring moment...

The Leafline Difference

Leafline stands apart because we treat unemployed youth as whole human beings... Leafline provides more than just a better CV – we offer renewed hope and purpose.

Current Collaborations and Future Expansion

Leafline already leverages local partnerships to amplify our work... By integrating with grassroots and digital partners, Leafline aims to reach even more young people.
