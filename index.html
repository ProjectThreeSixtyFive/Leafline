About Us

The Willow is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering elderly South Africans (ages 45-65) by providing essential resources to build ATS-friendly CVs, apply for jobs, and access valuable job market insights. We utilize AI tools integrated with the human touch to craft custom CVs tailored to South African standards, assisting individuals who may not have access to these resources or know-how. Our goal is to bridge the gap for seniors struggling to re-enter the workforce.

Resources

Our resources include step-by-step guides for creating ATS-friendly CVs, job application tips, and links to various government programs aimed at reducing unemployment among older South Africans. These resources are specifically tailored for seniors who may lack the necessary technical skills to navigate modern job application platforms.

How to Apply for Jobs

Steps to Apply on LinkedIn:

  1. Create a LinkedIn Profile: Ensure your profile is complete, including a professional photo and your updated CV.
  2. Search for Jobs: Use LinkedIn's job search tool to filter opportunities based on your skills, location, and industry.
  3. Customize Your Application: Tailor your CV and cover letter for each position.
  4. Connect with Recruiters: If possible, connect with the recruiter or hiring manager.

Steps to Apply Directly on Company Websites:

  1. Visit the Careers Page: Most companies have a 'Careers' section on their website. Look for jobs that match your skills.
  2. Prepare Your Documents: Upload your CV and cover letter in the required format.
  3. Track Your Applications: Use a spreadsheet to track all your applications and follow up if necessary.
