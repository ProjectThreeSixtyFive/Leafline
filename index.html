Vision & Mission

At *The Willow Leaf*, we believe in **empowering the elderly and disadvantaged** by providing AI-driven and personalized CV assistance. Our goal is to close the gap, ensuring that everyone has access to professional career support.

How We Help

We **combine AI and human expertise** to create ATS-optimized, visually appealing CVs. Our process includes:

  • AI-based keyword optimization to **pass ATS filters**.
  • Personalized styling – **colors, fonts, and layouts** tailored for each individual.
  • One-on-one support to **ensure clarity and confidence**.

Steps to Get Your CV

  1. Step 1: Fill out our questionnaire to provide details about your experience and goals.
  2. Step 2: We create an **AI-optimized** draft and personalize the design.
  3. Step 3: You review and request any changes.
  4. Step 4: We finalize the CV and send it to you.
