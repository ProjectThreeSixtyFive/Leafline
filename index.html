Executive Summary

LeafLine is a digital initiative aimed at bridging the employment information gap in South Africa. By aggregating and presenting up-to-date, official employment statistics and insights, LeafLine empowers job seekers, policymakers, and the general public with accessible and reliable data.

In South Africa, employment instability has an alarming impact on mental health, particularly among the youth. In a third world country like South Africa, where unemployment remains critically high, job insecurity fuels a deep sense of hopelessness, desperation, and imbalance within the society. Education no longer guarantees employment, leading to widespread frustration, diminished self-confidence, and a growing crisis of purpose. The instability in job opportunities doesn't just affect economic growth, but deeply affects the nation's collective mental health, dignity, and hope for the future. LeafLine aims to restore vision, provide hope, and be a beacon of information for those navigating these tough times.
