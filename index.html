Empowering Job Seekers and Businesses

The Willow is committed to bridging the technological gap for job seekers, particularly those aged 45-65. In today’s digital job market, many experienced professionals struggle with AI-driven hiring systems. We provide tools, resources, and personalized assistance to help them navigate this evolving landscape.

From AI-enhanced CV styling to in-depth guidance on Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), we ensure job seekers maximize their potential. We also focus on NGOs and businesses, offering AI solutions to enhance hiring strategies.

About The Willow

The Willow was founded on the belief that technology should empower, not hinder, job seekers. We specialize in helping individuals who feel left behind by modern hiring systems. Our mission is to provide personalized support, bridging the gap between talent and opportunity.

Key areas we focus on:

  • Helping job seekers understand AI-driven recruitment.
  • Providing free custom CV styling for individuals aged 45-65.
  • Supporting NGOs with employment-focused AI solutions.
  • Offering AI-assisted job-matching services.
