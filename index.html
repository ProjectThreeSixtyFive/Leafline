About Us

The Willow is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering elderly individuals (ages 45-65) to create professional CVs, apply for jobs, and find valuable resources to enhance their career opportunities.

Resources

Our resources include CV-building guides, job application tips, and links to various government programs aimed at reducing unemployment among older South Africans.
